Features of the college at a glance

  • Smoking and politics free campus.
  • The college is located in a very pleasant environment along the Chandpur-Laxmipur highway.
  • Managed by an efficient and experienced Governing Board.
  • Facilitated teaching through direct supervision by experienced and trained teaching staff.
  • Maximum scholarships and sub-scholarships are guaranteed.
  • Class Test (CT), Tutorial Test (TT) and Model Test (MT) are well organized.
  • Special group classes are taught to ensure A+ for meritorious students.
  • BNCC, Rover Scout and Red Crescent activities are ongoing.
  • There is a good arrangement of hostels and dormitories.
  • Well-equipped computer labs provide group-based training to students.
  • Science department students are ensured regular practical classes in well-equipped laboratories.
  • There is a rich library.
  • The results of the internal examination are declared through the parent assembly.
  • For BM (Business Management) stream students there is good provision of college supervised internship.
  • The higher secondary course is divided into 6 parts and exams are conducted in semester system.
  • Home visits are conducted under the supervision of the guide teacher to improve the quality of the student's studies.
  • Improved and low cost communication system with neighboring police stations and districts.
  • So poor and meritorious students are given special benefits based on the results of the examination conducted by the college.
  • With the aim of achieving mental excellence of the students, annual banquet, study tour and Organization of sports competitions.
  • Teaching in modern way with the help of multimedia and projector.
  • The result of each test is informed to the parents through mobile phones and SMS.
  • Debating and cultural programs are regular.

Code of conduct of students on college campus

  • Code of conduct of students on college campus
  • Every student should come to the college wearing the dress and identity card prescribed by the college authorities.
  • Students should be polite, courteous and polite.
  • Cannot stay in balcony, canteen or market during classes.
  • If you want to go outside/home before the class vacation or for any urgent need, you should go with the permission of the principal.
  • Political talk, meetings, smoking and carrying and use of mobile phones are prohibited in the college campus.
  • In case of absence even for one day due to any special reason, an application should be made to the principal as soon as possible along with the recommendation of the guardian along with appropriate evidence. Otherwise fine is imposed as per the rules of the institution.
  • Every day before entering the class the notification on the notice board should be seen.
  • If a student engages in such activities against the discipline of the college or malpractices in the examination, he is given Compulsory (TC).
  • It is recommended that the student attend the class at least 5 minutes before the start of the class.
  • Students have to keep their hair and nails short and cannot have bangles, bracelets, tattoos. Girls have to keep their nails short.
  • Nothing can be written on classroom benches, tables, black boards, walls, toilet doors etc.
  • No one other than parents or legal guardians will be allowed to visit the students during classes.
  • Exam admit cards, salary receipts, any kind of permit cannot be forged. Punishment for fraud is dismissal.
  • Carrying/use of Android mobile phones is prohibited in the college campus.
